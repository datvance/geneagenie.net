Home

Welcome to Geneagenie, where I share my genealogical research on my family tree, including the Vance, Scott, Heisig, Young and eventually the Moore and Polk branches.

I have only just begun the site and will keep adding information and photos, so check back often.  My grandparents were Andrew Milton Vance and Helen Heisig.  I have always felt that the histories of my ancestors were rich and interesting and wanted to share them for others who may be related and searching for family ties, or who just enjoy reading actual historical events about real people.

Feel free to comment or ask questions. I look forward to hearing from you.
